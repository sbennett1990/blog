## Tea Recipe

#### Ingredients

* 1tsp - 2tsp Turmeric
* 1tsp Cinnamon
* 1tsp Nutmeg
* pinch of Clove
* pinch of Black Pepper

* (Optional) 1tsp Ginger

* 1.5 cups Water
* Milk
* Honey

#### Steps

1. Simmer herbs and spices together in water, 10 minutes
2. Strain into cup, add Milk and Honey
